The following things occurred on this date. Space Shuttle Discovery completed its last-ever mission and made its final landing onto Kennedys runway at 11:57 am EST. The American Motors Corporation, best known for creating the Jeep, was bought out by Chrysler. Ford Mustang went on sale for the first time. Barbie was presented to the public for the first time. Bertha Landes became the first female mayor of a major city (Seattle) in the US. Dutch astronomer Johannes Fabricius discovered sunspots. Astronomer Nicolaus Copernicus recorded his first observation after witnessing a moon eclipse with a distant star. Today is also the birthday of Suga, Brittany Snow, Oscar Isaac, Yuri Gagarin.
